Research & Publications

An End-to-End Data Engineering Pipeline for AI-Assisted Project Domain Classification Using Multi-Source Data

Alaa Alhowaide · Ahmad Elaina · Ayoub Malkawi · Osama Almallah · Yaqoub Alothman

Designed and evaluated a full ETL pipeline ingesting 486 labeled records across 38 technical domains from heterogeneous sources (JSON, CSV, unstructured text). Benchmarked Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and BERT — detecting and resolving label leakage that boosted BERT macro F1 by 10× (0.05 → 0.52). Proposed a human-in-the-loop deployment strategy estimated to reduce manual classification labor by 75–80% in enterprise environments.
